## Axial Flux Electro Motor Animation Showcase

[https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=L4sYHeylYlo](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=L4sYHeylYlo)

**Client:** Mercdes Benz

**Agency:** Markgraph Agency

**Year:** 2023

**Technology Used:** Unreal Realtime Graphics

**Project Overview:**
In collaboration with Markgraph Agency, I developed an immersive animation project using Unreal Realtime Graphics to showcase the groundbreaking Axial Flux Electro Motor. This animation focused on the motor's novel coil design, a significant innovation in electric motor technology.

**Creative and Technical Execution:**
The animation was crafted to highlight the unique features of the Axial Flux Electro Motor, particularly its new coil design. Leveraging the power of Unreal Realtime Graphics, I created a visually stunning and highly detailed representation of the motor. The animation allowed viewers to explore the intricate components and understand how the new coil design enhances the motor's performance and efficiency.

**Educational and Visual Impact:**
The project aimed to educate viewers about the complexities and advantages of the Axial Flux Electro Motor in an engaging and accessible way.
