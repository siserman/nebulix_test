## Summary

With over 10 years of freelance experience as a realtime 3D artist, developer, and consultant, I have honed my skills and expertise in VR, AR, games, and 3D across various platforms and tools, such as Unity, Unreal, WebGL, and AI. I have worked on multiple award-winning projects for clients such as Volkswagen, Mercedes Benz, and Sunzinet, as well as created my own VR installation, The Pig Simulator, which received the media design award in 2017.
